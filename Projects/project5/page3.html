Superfoods Table Nutrients Benefits
Eggs Protein, Vitamins, and Minerals Eggs are considered a superfood due to their exceptional nutritional density, providing an excellent source of high-quality protein, essential vitamins such as B12 and D, minerals including selenium and phosphorus, and key antioxidants like lutein and zeaxanthin.
Avocados Healthy Fats and Vitamins Avocados are a super food that provides heart-healthy monounsaturated fats, ample fiber, and a diverse array of vitamins and minerals, including potassium and vitamin K.
Salmon Protein, Omega-3 Fatty Acids, and Vitamins Along with being a good source of protein its rich content of omega-3 fatty acids, which contribute to heart health, reduce inflammation, and supporting brain function makes it a superfood.
Sweet Potatoes Vitamins and Minerals Boasting a high content of antioxidants like beta-carotene that support vision and immune function, and their fiber-rich composition promotes digestive health and helps regulate blood sugar levels.
Mushrooms Vitamins and Minerals They are a rich source of essential nutrients, including B vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Known for their immune-boosting properties, mushrooms contribute to overall well-being, support bone health.
Berries Vitamins, Minerals, and Water A powerhouse of vitamins and minerals. their antioxidant capacity reduces risk of heart disease, cancer, and inflammation.
Broccoli Vitamins and Minerals Consists of high levels of vitamin C for immune support, fiber for digestive health, and antioxidants like sulforaphane that may have anti-cancer properties.
Spinach Vitamins and Minerals Packed with a variety of minerals such as Vitamins, it supports immune function and bone health. It also is dense in antioxidants.
Garlic Vitamins and Minearls It has an unique combination of sulfur compounds, antioxidants, and vitamins. It's known for its ability to support cardiovascular health, boost the immune system, and have anti-inflammatory properties.
Poultry Protein, Minerals, and Vitamins Poultry is a superfood due to its rich protein content, supporting muscle development and overall body function. Additionally, poultry offers essential vitamins, such as B vitamins and vitamin D, along with minerals like iron and zinc.

Key Take-Aways:

Exercising is crucial but pointless if you're not putting the right food into your body. Eating clean is simply avoiding processed foods and eating natural foods rich in nutrients. With consistent clean eating and exercise, you are on the inevitable path to a healthier and happier lifestyle.
